Classes

the term “Class” refers to the weapons and equipment chosen by the player that they carry throughout the Game. However not all classes are made equally if you choose the right class for your skills you can have a huge advantage in the warzone. Depending on the perks you have you can have up to two weapons one primary and one secondairy, the option range from slower but longer range and more powerfull snipers or quick close ranged submachine guns. all weapons have pros and cons, theese are some of my prefereed combinations


M13, Car98k

One such class is the M13 and the Car98k i personnaly choose this class as the M13 can cover close and medium range gunfights well with a very high rate of fire, I take the longer range sniper (Car98k) to take out opnents quickly as one shot to the head is an instant down in warzone
